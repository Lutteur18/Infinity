Frequently Asked Questions

Can I install a tank if I have a small yard?

Yes! Our slim and vertical tanks are designed for tight spaces and can fit alongside walls or in corners.

Do you offer installation advice?

Absolutely. Our team will help you choose the best tank and provide installation tips for your unique space.

What sizes are available?

We offer tanks from 500L to 10,000L, including compact models for small spaces and larger options for greater storage needs.

Are your tanks UV resistant?

Yes, all our tanks are made from UV-resistant, food-grade polyethylene to ensure long-lasting durability in the sun.

Do you deliver and install nationwide?

We deliver and install tanks across South Africa, making it easy for you to get set up wherever you are.

Can I use your tanks for rainwater harvesting?

Yes, our tanks are perfect for rainwater harvesting and can be easily connected to your gutter system.
